Potato Soup

Soup season is here! Time to break out that crockpot!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Chicken Broth
  • Garlic
  • Heavy Cream
  • Cooked Bacon, Cheese, Sour Cream, and Chives

Steps

  1. Add potatoes, onion, chicken broth, and garlic to a crockpot.
  2. Cook on low for 8 hours.
  3. Add in heavy cream.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve topped with bacon, cheese, sour cream, and chives.
