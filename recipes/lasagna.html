Lasagna

Full of pasta, meat sauce, and melty cheese this is the world's best lasagna just like grandma used to make!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Italian Sausage
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef and italian sausage and drain grease.
  2. Mix cooked meat and marinara sauce.
  3. In a bowl, mix Riotta and Mozzarella cheeses together.
  4. In a baking pan, layer the meat sauce followed by the cheese mixture and finally lasagna noodles.
  5. Repeat until the pan is full.
  6. Bake at 450 for 30 minutes.
