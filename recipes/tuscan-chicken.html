Tuscan Chicken

The call it "Marry Me Chicken" for a good reason.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Heavy Cream
  • Sun-dried Tomatoes
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper and brown in a skillet.
  2. Remove chicken from skillet and add sun-dried tomatoes and garlic, sauteeing until fragrant.
  3. Add heavy cream to the skillet and cook until reduced.
  4. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  5. Serve over pasta.
